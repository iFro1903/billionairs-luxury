5. Head Tag Ordering Fixed

Fix Pages
Moved meta charset + viewport to top of head (before any scripts/styles) index, login, create-account, dashboard, demo
Before
<head> <link rel="stylesheet" ...> <script src="..."></script> <meta charset="UTF-8"> <meta name="viewport" ...>
After
<head> <meta charset="UTF-8"> <meta name="viewport" ...> <link rel="stylesheet" ...> <script src="..." defer></script>
Erwarteter Lighthouse-Effekt: First Contentful Paint (FCP) verbessert sich um ca. 0.5-1s. Render-blocking Ressourcen reduziert von ~15 auf ~3 (nur kritische CSS). Keine visuellen Aenderungen am Design -- nur schnelleres Laden.
